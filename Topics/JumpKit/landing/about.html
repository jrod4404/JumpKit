About

Stop searching. Start jumping.

JumpKit was born out of a simple frustration: we spend too much time looking for things we've already found.

Every day, knowledge workers navigate the same web links, the same shared folders, the same internal resources — over and over. Some live in browser bookmarks. Some in chat messages. Some in email signatures. Some in Notion docs. The result is wasted time, fragmented workflows, and the constant question: "Where did I put that link?"

We built JumpKit to be the answer. A single, lightweight desktop app that sits on your machine and gives you one-click access to anywhere — web links, local folders, network shares, and team resources — all organized, searchable, and shareable.

Our mission: give people their time back. Every unnecessary click, every folder dive, every "let me find that link" message — that's time stolen from real work. JumpKit tracks the savings so you can see exactly how much it matters.

JumpKit is built for individuals, small teams, and organizations who value speed and simplicity. No bloat. No unnecessary features. Just a fast, clean launcher that gets out of your way.
