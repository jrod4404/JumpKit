Features

Everything at your fingertips

Built for single users, teams, and enterprises who are tired of wasting time navigating, and want their data local.

Instant Jump Launcher

Store web links, local directories, and file paths in organized categories. Click once to jump — no drilling through folders or bookmarks.

Categories & Filters

Organize jumps into drag-ready categories, mark favorites, and filter views (Active, Favorites, Recent, Archive) so you always see the right jumps at the right time.
