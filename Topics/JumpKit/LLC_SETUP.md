## 🏢 Michigan LLC Setup — Step by Step

### Step 1: Choose Your LLC Name (5 min)
- Pick a name that's broad enough to cover future businesses
- Check availability: https://cofs.lara.state.mi.us/SearchApi/Search/Search
- Rules: must include "LLC" or "L.L.C.", can't duplicate existing names

### Step 2: File Articles of Organization (~15 min + $50)
- File online: https://www.michigan.gov/lara/bureau-list/bcs/corps
- Cost: $50 one-time filing fee
- You'll need: LLC name, registered agent (can be yourself), your address, member names
- Processing: same-day online, ~5-7 days by mail

### Step 3: Get a Registered Agent
- Can be yourself (home address becomes public record) OR
- Use a service like **Northwest Registered Agent** (~$125/yr) — keeps home address private
- Recommended if you don't want your address publicly searchable

### Step 4: Get Your EIN (5 min, free)
- IRS EIN application: https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online
- Free, instant, no accountant needed
- This is your business's "Social Security Number" — needed for bank accounts + taxes

### Step 5: Create an Operating Agreement (~30 min)
- Michigan doesn't legally require it but you absolutely want one
- Single-member LLC = simple 1-page doc that proves you and the LLC are separate entities
- Free templates: Northwest Registered Agent, LegalZoom, or Max can draft one
- Sign it, keep it — never needs to be filed with the state

### Step 6: Open a Business Bank Account

| Bank | Why | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| **Mercury** (recommended) | Free, online, built for startups, no minimum balance, great UI | mercury.com — apply online in 10 min |
| Relay | Free, multiple sub-accounts (great for separating revenue/taxes) | relay.fi |
| Chase Business | Brick-and-mortar, sign-up bonuses ($300-500 often available) | Requires in-person visit |

What you'll need to open:
- EIN (Step 4)
- Articles of Organization (Step 2)
- Operating Agreement (Step 5)
- Personal ID
- Initial deposit (Mercury/Relay = $0 minimum; Chase = varies)

### Step 7: Update Everything Under the LLC
- **Lemon Squeezy** → Settings → Payouts → update business name + EIN
- **Apple Developer account** → update entity name (triggers installer rebuild + re-notarization)
- Keep personal and business finances 100% separate from day one — this is what protects you legally
