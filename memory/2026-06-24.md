## JumpKit ghost team jumps root cause — 2026-06-24
- Root cause found: `saveRecoverySnapshot` was saving ALL jumps including team jumps (jumps with a teamId). On restore after login, those team jumps got re-written to SQLite, building up 80 ghost entries over multiple import/logout cycles.
- Fix: recovery snapshot and restore now filter to personal-only (teamId == null). Local fallback `_persistJump` also filters out team jumps.
- SQLite cleaned: deleted 80 ghost team jumps, leaving 27 active personal + 14-15 real team jumps = 40 total.
- Stale localStorage snapshot key: `jk_recovery_snapshot_6c0763f4-21d4-470a-bc10-1e6eafa3873a` — Jeff needed to run `localStorage.removeItem(...)` in DevTools console once to clear old snapshot. Returns `undefined` = success.
- After cleanup: 40 jumps (25 personal + 15 team). Home page confirmed correct.
